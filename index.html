current projects.

onSet - Create Profiles for Creative Professionals
onSet is an online marketplace for professionals/freelancers in the creative trades (hair stylists, colourists, makeup artists, personal stylists, etc). Its main objectives are to create a space for professionals to showcase their amazing work, to build new clientele, and for potential employers to view what others have thought of working with each professional through the review system.
onSet was presented at DecodeMTL's demo day on August 26th, 2016.
The Stack
Front End: React, jQuery, Auth0Lock, CSS3, Flexbox, and Bootstrap.
Back End: Node.js, Express, Nodemailer, MySQL & the Instagram API
